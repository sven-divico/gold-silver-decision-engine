# Product Thesis

## Working title
Gold & Silver Decision Engine

## Why this product exists
Private investors in Germany face a non-trivial decision when comparing physical gold and physical silver.

The key frictions include:
- VAT treatment differences
- differing volatility profiles
- distinct macro behavior of gold and silver
- the gold/silver ratio as a potentially useful relative signal
- the challenge of translating market data into practical investor decisions

Most available information is fragmented. The goal of this app is to turn the topic into a more structured, decision-oriented tool.

## Primary user problem
A user wants to understand:
- whether gold or silver currently looks more attractive
- how silver VAT affects entry and exit economics in Germany
- how historical cycle patterns behaved
- whether the gold/silver ratio is stretched, neutral, or compressed
- what the user’s scenario looks like under simplified assumptions

## Initial value proposition
The app should help a user:
1. compare gold and silver on a scenario basis
2. understand the VAT handicap of silver in Germany
3. inspect the gold/silver ratio
4. view historical context in a simple visual way
5. derive a basic interpretation from rule-based signals

## Design principle
This is not a trading terminal. It is a lean analytical web app with clear assumptions and transparent logic.

## Scope discipline
The first version should not try to:
- automate trading
- predict prices
- provide personalized financial advice
- support user accounts
- become a broad portfolio tracker

## Product tone
- analytical
- calm
- transparent
- assumption-driven
- not promotional
